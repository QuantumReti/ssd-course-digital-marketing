# Digital Marketing Mastery - Sun State Digital Agent Training

Complete digital marketing knowledge base for Mason and all AI agents at Sun State Digital.
Covers every channel and strategy needed to advise Brisbane SMB clients with confidence.

## Modules
- 01 Local SEO Fundamentals
- 02 Google Ads for SMBs
- 03 Social Media for Local Business
- 04 Content Marketing
- 05 Email Marketing
- 06 Analytics and Reporting

## Philosophy
Digital marketing for small businesses is not about impressions and brand awareness. It is about getting the phone to ring, the form to be filled, and the booking to be made. Every strategy in this course is evaluated through one lens: does it bring clients to the door?
