# Module 03: Social Media for Local Business

## Which Platforms Matter for Brisbane SMBs

### Facebook - Priority 1 for Most Brisbane SMBs
Audience: 35-55 year olds. Homeowners. Decision-makers. Community group members.

Why it works: Brisbane has incredibly active local Facebook community groups. People ask who is a good plumber in Chermside and get flooded with recommendations. Being active on Facebook means being part of that conversation.

Investment: 4 posts per week + active community group presence

### Instagram - Priority 2
Audience: 25-40 year olds. More visual industries (cafes, food, beauty, renovation).
Why it works: Visual-first platform ideal for showcasing work (before and after renovations, food photography, fitness results).
Investment: 5 posts per week (3 posts + 2 Reels)

### Google Business Profile Posts - Priority 1 (Often Overlooked)
Audience: Everyone searching for your business on Google.
Why it works: GBP posts appear directly in search results and Maps. Most businesses never post here - giving active businesses a massive advantage.
Investment: 2 posts per week minimum

### TikTok - Optional, High Reward for Right Businesses
Audience: Under 35.
Why it works: Surprisingly high organic reach. Trades with entertaining content (electricians, plumbers, builders) can go viral locally.
Investment: Only if the business owner is willing to create short-form video content.

### LinkedIn - Low Priority for Most Brisbane SMBs
Relevant for: B2B services (accountants, IT, consultants).
Skip for: Trade businesses, hospitality, consumer health.

## Content Pillars for Service Businesses

All social media content should fit into one of five pillars:

### Pillar 1: Before and After (30% of content)
The single most engaging content type for service businesses. The transformation creates emotional engagement and proves competence.

Examples:
- Plumber: Blocked drain before / clear pipes after
- Dentist: Before Invisalign / 6 months later (with consent)
- Landscaper: Overgrown backyard to outdoor entertaining space
- Cafe: Empty Monday morning to packed brunch line

Caption formula:
[Problem description] - this came in last week for [business type/suburb]. [Brief process]. The result? [Outcome description]. If you have got [similar problem], give us a call - we sort these out fast.

### Pillar 2: Behind the Scenes (20% of content)
Humanises the business. Shows the real people, the real work, the real day-to-day.

Examples:
- The team heading out for a big day in [suburb]
- Setting up for service - here is what our morning prep looks like
- End of a big week - X jobs completed, Y happy customers

### Pillar 3: Tips and Education (20% of content)
Demonstrates expertise. Provides genuine value. Attracts people who are researching.

Examples:
- 3 signs your hot water system is about to fail (and what to do about it)
- Why your Google listing might be hurting your business without you knowing
- The number one mistake Brisbane cafes make on Instagram

### Pillar 4: Social Proof (20% of content)
Reviews, testimonials, shoutouts.

Examples:
- Screenshot of a 5-star Google review
- Another happy customer in [suburb]
- Case study post: We helped [business type] go from [before] to [after]

### Pillar 5: Promotions and Offers (10% of content)
Direct promotional content. Keep this to 10%.

Examples:
- This week only: [service] for  - [X] spots available
- We are running a special for new customers in [suburb] this month

## Posting Frequency Guide

| Platform | Minimum | Ideal |
|----------|---------|-------|
| Facebook | 3x/week | 4-5x/week |
| Instagram | 3x/week | 5x/week (3 posts + 2 Reels) |
| Google Business Profile | 1x/week | 2x/week |
| TikTok | 2x/week | Daily |

Consistency beats volume. 3 posts per week every week beats 10 posts one week and nothing the next.

## Reels and Short Video Without a Production Team

Equipment: Phone (iPhone or Android) + natural light
Length: 15-45 seconds
Format: Start with a hook, show the work, end with a CTA

Hook examples:
- Watch us clear a 10-year blocked drain in 30 seconds
- This is what we found inside this pipe
- Before and after - 2 weeks of work in 10 seconds

Editing: Use CapCut (free) or Instagram's built-in editor. Add text overlays and music.

Posting tip: Reels perform best on Instagram and Facebook when posted between 7-9am or 6-8pm on Tuesday through Thursday.

## Hashtag Strategy for Brisbane

Three-Layer Hashtag Formula:

Layer 1 - Location (2-3 hashtags):
#brisbane #brisbanebusiness #southeastqueensland #sunshinecoast

Layer 2 - Industry (3-4 hashtags):
#brisbanebuilder #brisbaneplumber #brisbanecafe #brisbanedentist

Layer 3 - Niche/Specific (3-5 hashtags):
#emergencyplumber #gasfitting #brisbanehomestyling #cafebrisbanecbd

Total: 8-12 hashtags on Instagram. 3-5 on Facebook.

Avoid: Giant generic hashtags like #love or #instagood - far too competitive and irrelevant.

## Responding to Comments and DMs

The golden rule: Reply within 1 hour during business hours.

Why it matters:
- Facebook and Instagram algorithm rewards accounts with high engagement rates
- DMs are often sales opportunities - a slow response loses the lead
- Visible fast replies signal to other followers that the business cares

Response Templates:

Positive comment:
Thank you so much [Name]! Really appreciate the kind words. If you ever need us again, you know where we are.

Question in comments:
Great question! [Answer briefly]. For more detail, feel free to DM us or give us a call - happy to help.

Negative comment:
Hi [Name], we are really sorry to hear this. This is not the experience we want for anyone. Can you please DM us your details so we can sort this out directly? We want to make it right.

## Boosting Posts vs Running Ads

Boost a Post When:
- The post is performing well organically (high likes, comments, shares)
- You want to extend reach for brand or social proof content
- Budget is small (10-30 boost)
- Goal is awareness and engagement, not direct leads

Run a Proper Facebook/Instagram Ad When:
- Goal is direct leads or bookings
- You want precise audience targeting (suburb-level, age, interests, homeowner status)
- You are promoting a specific offer with a CTA
- Budget is 20+ per day

For Brisbane SMBs - typical setup:
- Target: 10km radius from business + specific suburb targeting
- Age: 28-60
- Interests: Homeowners, recent movers, home improvement (for tradies)
- Ad type: Single image or video with clear offer and phone number/CTA

## Metrics That Matter

| Metric | What It Means | Target |
|--------|--------------|--------|
| Reach | Unique people who saw your content | Growing month-on-month |
| Engagement Rate | Interactions divided by Reach | 2%+ good, 5%+ excellent |
| Link Clicks | Clicks to website or booking page | Increasing trend |
| DMs Generated | New conversations started | Track weekly |
| Profile Visits | People checking out the business | Increasing after posts |

What NOT to focus on:
- Follower count (especially bought followers - worse than useless)
- Likes on individual posts (weak signal)
- Impressions in isolation (only matters with context of CTR)
