# Module 04: Content Marketing

## Why Content Matters for Brisbane SMBs

Content marketing - primarily blog posts and service pages - is the foundation of long-term organic SEO. Google rewards websites that consistently publish relevant, local, useful content.

The caveat: content marketing is a slow burn. Expect 3-6 months before significant traffic gains. But the compounding effect means it gets stronger over time - unlike ads, which stop working the moment you stop paying.

---

## Blog Topics That Rank for Service Businesses

### Template 1: [Service] in [Suburb] Pages
These are high-converting, local-intent pages. Create one for every suburb you serve.

Examples:
- Plumber in Chermside
- Dentist in Aspley
- Landscaping Services in The Gap
- Cafe in Newmarket

Minimum content per page: 400-600 words covering: what the service includes, why location matters, your specific expertise in that area, reviews from that area, and a clear CTA.

### Template 2: Cost/Price Pages
People researching services almost always want to know the cost. Owning these searches captures highly qualified prospects.

Examples:
- How Much Does a Plumber Cost in Brisbane?
- Dental Implant Cost Brisbane 2024
- Landscaping Prices Brisbane - What to Expect

Content approach: Give real price ranges with context. Do not be vague. Specificity builds trust and captures long-tail traffic.

### Template 3: Best [Service] Near Me and Comparison Pages
Captures comparison shoppers and review seekers.

Examples:
- Best Plumbers in Brisbane's North - What to Look For
- Top 5 Things to Ask Your Brisbane Electrician Before Hiring
- How to Choose a Dentist in Brisbane

### Template 4: How-To and Educational Content
Captures people early in the research phase. Builds brand awareness.

Examples:
- How to Tell if Your Hot Water System Needs Replacing
- 5 Signs You Need a New Website (And What to Do Next)
- Why Your Brisbane Business Is Not Showing Up on Google

Conversion tip: End every educational post with a soft CTA: If you would like a professional assessment, we offer [free/low-cost entry point] - contact us here.

## Writing for Humans AND Google

Write like you talk. Avoid stiff, corporate language. Use contractions. Ask questions. Vary sentence length.

Robotic: Our comprehensive plumbing solutions encompass a full spectrum of residential and commercial services delivered by our expert team of licensed professionals.

Human (and better for SEO): We handle everything plumbing-related - from blocked drains and burst pipes to full bathroom renovations. Our plumbers are licensed, local, and on call 7 days a week.

### Question-Based Subheadings
Using H2/H3 headings as questions increases the chance of appearing in featured snippets (position zero).

Examples:
- How Much Does Emergency Plumbing Cost in Brisbane?
- What Is Included in a Website SEO Package?
- How Long Does a Dental Implant Take?

### Local References
Weave in suburb names, local landmarks, local events, and Brisbane-specific context.

Example:
Whether you are in Chermside, Aspley, or out toward Stafford Heights, our plumbers are typically on-site within the hour. We know Brisbane's north like the back of our hand - we have been working in these suburbs for over a decade.

## Content Repurposing Framework

Create content once and use it in multiple formats.

The Repurposing Chain:
1. Write the blog post (500-800 words, SEO-optimised)
2. Summarise as a social post (Facebook/Instagram - pull the key point + CTA)
3. Turn into an email (Send to existing client list - add a personal opener)
4. Create a Google Business Profile post (120-word version, link back to the blog)
5. Record a short Reel or TikTok (Speak the key 3 points to camera - casual and natural)

One piece of content creates 5 touchpoints with minimal additional effort.

### Example Repurpose in Action

Blog post: Why Your Brisbane Plumbing Business Is Not Showing Up on Google

Facebook post:
Spent an hour looking at plumbing businesses online this week. 7 out of 10 were not showing up on Google properly. If you are a Brisbane plumber not ranking, it is usually one of these 3 things... [link to post]

Email:
Hey [Name] - wrote something this week you might find useful. Lots of Brisbane tradies are invisible on Google without knowing why. Here is what I found...

GBP Post:
If your plumbing business is not ranking on Google, the fix might be simpler than you think. We have written a guide to the top 3 reasons local businesses miss out. Read it here: [link]

Reel:
Three reasons your Brisbane trade business is not showing up on Google - number two surprises most people... (30 seconds, to camera)

## AI-Assisted Content Creation

AI tools can dramatically speed up content creation. Here is how to use them effectively.

Effective prompt structure:
Write a 600-word blog post for a Brisbane plumbing business targeting the keyword plumber in Chermside. The tone should be friendly and conversational. Include: what services they offer in Chermside, why choosing a local plumber matters, a section on response times, 3 customer-friendly headings, and end with a soft CTA to call or book online. The business is called Brisbane Plumbing and Gas.

What AI does well:
- Drafting structure and flow
- Generating heading options
- Creating variations for different suburbs
- Writing meta descriptions and title tags

What humans must add:
- Real client stories and examples
- Specific local references (just off Gympie Road, near Westfield Chermside)
- Brand voice and personality
- Accuracy checks on pricing, service details, and claims

Rule: Never publish AI content without a human review. AI drafts; humans finalise.

## Internal Linking Strategy

Internal links help Google understand your site structure and distribute authority across pages.

Every blog post should link to:
- The primary service page it relates to
- 1-2 other relevant blog posts

Every service page should link to:
- The contact/quote page
- Related service pages
- Relevant blog content

Example for a plumbing site:
Page: Blocked Drains Brisbane
Links to:
- Emergency Plumbing Brisbane (service page)
- How to Tell if You Have a Blocked Drain (blog)
- Contact Us / Get a Quote (conversion page)
- Plumbing Services [related suburbs] (geo pages)
